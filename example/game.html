Strike The Dark

This game was made for the Godot Wild Jam 72

Controls:

Movement - WASD, Dash - Space, Shoot - LMB, Charge Laser - RMB


Upgrades:

Broken Prism : lets you harness the light to shoot bullets, but only when you're on the light

Full Prism: Upgrades your bullet's damage

Light Pack: Let's you charge light into a backpack to shoot outside of the light and cast a dangerous light ray

Description:

  Fight against waves of monsters in order to save the light, you can buy items

and upgrades in the duck's shop in order to be able to survive harder waves
